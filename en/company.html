Management

Juan Carlos Göldy

Co-Founder & CEO

A pioneer in the Latam digital advertising market, Juan Carlos has a broad background of over more than 20 years in companies like Tutopia.com and InZearch which he founded and then sold to Newscorp Group, to eventually became the top online media company in Latin America. Juan Carlos remained at .FOX for 7 years, leading the regional operations and leading Facebook ́s and LinkedIn ́s business development in the region.

He is the CEO of Logan and also serves as the current Argentina’s Chairman and a Latin American board member of the Mobile Marketing Association.

Ignacio Alvarez Saez

Co-Founder & President

Started off as Creative Director for leading global brands like Sony, McDonald ́s, Bank of America and Air France. Thus he was recruited by Gameloft, largest world mobile games developer, to steer the marketing of this powerhouse in Latam. Then founded Convoy, digital agency based in France and Latin America.

At Logan Ignacio, an active speaker at industry summits, is focused in accelerating business growth and product and service excellence while, obsessed with productivity, manages the Sales, Innovation, Interactive Experiences and Marketing teams.

Manuel Tomé
